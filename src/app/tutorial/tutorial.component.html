What is JResume?

Simply put, JResume is an online resume generator.

Advantages

  1. It's much easier to create a resume here than in Microsoft Word.
  2. You can choose from a variety of templates to use when generating your resume.
  3. This allows you to separate the content of your resume from its layout/design.
  4. Tired of your current theme? You can just select a new one and redownload your resume! It's so much easier to change the layout of your resume here than in Microsoft Word.
  5. You can easily download both a PDF and an HTML (webpage) version of your resume.
  6. Want to put your resume on a website? The HTML version of your resume is just like any other webpage and it has all the css/js/image/fonts bundled into the .html file, so all you need to do is upload it to your web server!
  7. Completely free and open source. Don't have the funds to pay for one of those premium online resume generators? That's fine, because we won't force you to pay a thing. Although, if you can, please donate :)
  8. Some online resume generators require you to pay to remove their branded watermark from your resume. We don't do any such thing.

How Do I Use This?

Step 1: Click Edit

Step 2: Fill Out Personal Information

Step 3: Expand And Fill Out Other Sections

Step 4: Save Your Progress So You Can Edit Your Resume Again Later

Step 5: Select a Theme

Step 6: Preview Results And Download Resume

Step 7: Donate If Satisfied
